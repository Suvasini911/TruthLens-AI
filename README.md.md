##### **# TruthLens AI**



###### \## Verify Before You Trust



TruthLens AI is an AI-powered misinformation and credibility verification platform designed to help users assess the trustworthiness of online content before sharing or consuming it.



The system analyzes text content and source URLs, calculates a trust score, evaluates risk levels, provides transparent explanations, and generates downloadable verification reports.



\---





##### **## Problem Statement**



The rapid growth of digital media has increased the spread of misinformation, fake news, and manipulated content across online platforms.



Users often struggle to determine whether information is trustworthy before sharing it. TruthLens AI addresses this challenge by providing a simple, explainable, and user-friendly credibility verification system.



\---





##### **## ASEEV Framework**



TruthLens AI follows the ASEEV Framework:



###### \### A – Analyze Content

Identifies suspicious keywords and content patterns.



###### \### S – Score Credibility

Calculates an overall trust score.



###### \### E – Evaluate Risk

Classifies content as Low, Medium, or High Risk.



###### \### E – Explain Findings

Provides transparent explanations for results.



###### \### V – Verify Information

Generates a downloadable verification report.
